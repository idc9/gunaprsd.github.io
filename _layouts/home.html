Research Interests
I am interested in developing and analyzing statistical algorithms for non-standard data such as: networks, text and high-dimensional data. My research draws on probability, optimization, and statistics/machine learning. My work is currently driven by problems coming from neuroscience and the law.
  • Support Vector Machine behavior in high-dimensional settings
  • Large scale network and text analysis of the legal citaion network
  • Dimensionality reduction for network valued data
Brief Bio
